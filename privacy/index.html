Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-20

This Privacy Policy describes how Twain SMS (“we,” “us”) collects and uses information when you text our phone number to interact with our SMS conversational service (the “Service”).

Information we collect

  • Your phone number and message content you send us.
  • Message metadata such as timestamps, carrier, and delivery status.
  • If you send a photo, we receive the media and related metadata.

How we use information

  • To respond to your messages and operate the Service.
  • To improve reliability and quality, including debugging, abuse prevention, and aggregate analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

How we share information

  • We use service providers (for example, an SMS provider and cloud infrastructure) to deliver and operate the Service.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the Service from abuse.

Data retention

We retain messages and logs only as long as needed to operate the Service, resolve issues, and comply with legal requirements. You may request deletion as described below.

Your choices

  • Opt out: Reply STOP to stop receiving messages.
  • Help: Reply HELP for help.
  • Data requests: Contact support@YOURDOMAIN.com to request access or deletion.
